Research Interests

My work lies in the broad area of topology, with a focus on the interplay between geometric structures and homotopical methods. I investigate spaces that classify manifolds, explore characteristic invariants of fiber bundles, and analyze deformation spaces of embeddings and diffeomorphisms. My research draws on tools from higher category theory, calculus of functors and the role of K-theory in geometric and smooth settings.

For more on my academic background and publications, please see my CV.
